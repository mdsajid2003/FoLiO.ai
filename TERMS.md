## 6. Service Description

6.1. FoLiOAI is a **financial reconciliation and diagnostic tool** designed for Indian e-commerce sellers. The Service provides:

- (a) **Settlement Reconciliation:** Parsing and analysis of Amazon India Settlement Report V2 and Flipkart Settlement Reports to identify revenue, expenses, fees, and discrepancies;
- (b) **Revenue Leakage Detection:** Identification of potential overcharges, duplicate fees, missing reimbursements, weight slab errors, and incorrect referral fees;
- (c) **Tax Analysis:** GST summary (CGST/SGST/IGST breakdowns, slab-wise analysis, ITC estimates), TCS summary (Section 52 CGST Act), TDS summary (Section 194-O Income Tax Act), and income tax estimation (new regime, Section 44AD presumptive taxation);
- (d) **Recovery Actions:** Prioritized list of actionable steps to recover identified leakage, including support ticket templates;
- (e) **AI-Assisted Features:** Conversational AI assistant and narrative report generation powered by third-party AI models;
- (f) **Data Quality Assessment:** Reporting on data completeness, parsing accuracy, and assumptions used in calculations.

6.2. The Service processes your uploaded Settlement Data on our servers, generates analytical outputs, and returns these outputs to your browser. Processed reports may also be stored in your browser's local storage for your convenience.
